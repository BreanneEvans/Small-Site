NIALL

HOMETOWN:

Mullingar, Ireland

HIS START:

As a teenager he performed at
the Mullingar Arts Centre in a
fundraiser for the local football
team. Niall competed in season
7 of The X Factor, where he was
grouped with his bandmates.

ONE DIRECTION:

Niall became a singer and
songwriter for One Direction
and released five albums from
2011 to 2015, which hit number
one across the globe.

SOLO ALBUMS:

Niall dropped his debut solo
album, Flicker, in 2017.
After spending much of 2018 on
tour, he released Heartbreak
Weather in 2020.

CHARITY:

In 2016 Niall brought golf and
music together with Modest!
Golf Management to raise
money for Cancer research.

  • He wrote "Back For You"
