HARRY

HOMETOWN:

Cheshire, England

HIS START:

In high-school, Harry was in a
band called "White Eskimo."
Harry competed in season 7 of
The X Factor, where he was
grouped with his bandmates.

ONE DIRECTION:

Harry became a singer
and songwriter for One Direction
and released five albums from
2011 to 2015, which hit number
one across the globe.

SOLO ALBUMS:

His first solo album was
released in 2017, and his
second Album, Fine Line
was released in 2019.

ACTING:

He made his movie debut as a
soldier in Christopher Nolan’s
Oscar-winning epic Dunkirk.
